Sherman Dining Hall

  • Call the Food Service Director
  • Specials
  • Outside BBQ Dinner
  • Opening Day Meal
  • Caribbean Meal
  • 100 Plates Across Campus

Sherman Dining Hall offers traditional all-you-care-to-eat dining and features both Glatt Kosher and non-Kosher foods.


Enjoy newly designed menus using the freshest ingredients cooked to order while you wait. New digital nutritional signs are displayed at stations to assist in making healthier choices.
You will also enjoy some fun festive events as well as all of your favorite tried and true stations featuring pizza, deli, grilled items and traditional fare.
There is a Gluten Free Corner that features Udi's breads, complete meal options, and dessert! For more allergen and nutritional information, please click here.

Kosher Dining


Brandeis University Dining Services recognizes the needs unique to our Kosher students. That is why we have done our best to provide the same variety and menu options available to Kosher students as are available to non-Kosher students.

Kosher eXpress


The Kosher eXpress convenience store features Pre-Made Sandwiches and Salads, Bottled Beverages, including Dr. Brown's, Microwavable Soups and Meals, Chips, Candy, Snacks and much, much more. Kosher eXpress accepts Points, Cash, WhoCash, MasterCard and Visa. We want to hear from you! Send an e-mail and let us know what types of items you'd like to see in the Kosher eXpress.
